To make the image clickable in Medium you need to follow these steps:

  1. Copy the link: https://donorbox.org/scottkelly
  2. Select the image (green border appears)
  3. Press ctrl+K for Windows or cmd+K on Mac
  4. Paste the link in the bubble that appears
